CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE

Padre Damaso Padre Damaso is a man full of greed and pride. His sensitive ego oftens results in him trying to defend himself too much.
Maria Clara Maria Clara is represented as a beautiful, humble, and reserved woman in the story, often being depicted as the ideal Filipino woman.
Elias Elias is a man aiding Ibarra after having been saved by him. He was also working on improving our society.
Cristostomo Ibarra Cristostomo Ibarra is the main character in our story. He is depicted as an independent, kind-hearted and civil man.
